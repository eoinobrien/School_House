Situated near Carlow, The School House offers accommodation within a converted 20th century property. Featuring eclectic antique furniture and free onsite parking, it is in the middle of an early Christian settlement, with a round tower and Celtic crosses.

Each room includes a seating area and a work desk. The private bath or shower rooms offers a hairdryer and bathrobes.

Other facilities offered at the family-run School House include a shared lounge, luggage storage, and laundry facilities. The county town of Port Laoise is just 40 minutes' drive away. The County Carlow Military Museum is less than 20 minutes' drive away.
