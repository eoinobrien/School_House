Perfect for families or groups, Self Catering at The School House provides guests with the perfect accommodation for any holiday.

With four beds, a fully fitted kitchen, bathroom and a living room with a stove. Self Catering provides everything one may need to be a perfect starting point to experience Ireland's East.

The Self Catering Accommodation also has access to the gardens and ponds of the School House, and are invited to make use of the available Barbecue.

Carlow and it's many attractions including Duckett's Grove, Brownshill Dolmen and the Altamont Gardens are just a short drive away.

Kildare and all it has to offer including The Curragh, the Irish National Stud and Gardens, the Newbridge Silverware Visitor Center are all nearby.

Port Laoise is just 40 minutes' drive away. With Dublin and all it has to offer a short drive also.
